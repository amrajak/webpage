PhD Scholar in Computational and Data Sciences

I am pursuing a Ph.D. under Dr. Deepak Subramani in the Computational and Data Sciences Department, IISc Bangalore. My research focuses on Topological Data Analysis (TDA), combining pure mathematical concepts from topology with the analysis of high-dimensional data.
